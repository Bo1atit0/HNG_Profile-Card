About Me

I’m an aspiring front-end developer passionate about creating clean, responsive, and user-friendly designs. I enjoy experimenting with CSS animations, gradients, and modern UI patterns to bring creativity into my projects. Beyond coding, I’m also a fashion designer, which influences my sense of design and attention to detail.
My main goal in this program is to become confident in building real-world, responsive web applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. I also want to strengthen my problem-solving and debugging skills so I can take on freelance projects and eventually land a paid internship.
I sometimes struggle with debugging complex code and fully understanding advanced JavaScript logic. I also find backend related concepts a bit challenging since my main interest lies in frontend development. However, I’m committed to improving through consistent practice and mentorship.
Keep going, even when things feel tough. Remember how far you’ve come, from writing your first line of HTML to understanding frameworks like React. Stay consistent, keep learning, and don’t be afraid to ask for help. You’re building something meaningful, one project at a time.
This journey hasn’t been easy, but it’s been rewarding. I’ve learned that progress comes with patience and consistency. I’m proud of how much I’ve grown and excited about where this path will lead next.
